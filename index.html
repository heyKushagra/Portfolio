// 06 — Case Study
Brand Identity & Packaging Design

Lunar Coffee

A conceptual specialty coffee brand

Project Overview

Lunar Coffee is a conceptual specialty coffee brand inspired by the calm, mysterious beauty of the moon. The goal was to create a premium visual identity that feels modern, minimal, and memorable.

This project explores the complete branding process—from defining the brand personality to designing a cohesive packaging system. Every design decision was focused on creating a distinctive and premium coffee experience.
